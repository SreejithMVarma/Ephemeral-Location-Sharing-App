CRITICAL · CANVAS
SVG-equivalent CustomPaint — not widget grid
RadarPainter draws: 4 concentric rings (stroke 0.5px, opacity 0.08–0.15), 4 crosshair lines + 2 diagonal lines (0.04 opacity), rotating sweep line with trail arc. AnimationController 0→2π at 4s. RepaintBoundary isolates canvas from UI rebuilds.
CRITICAL · BLIPS
Avatar blips: ring + fill + initials + idle pulse
Each blip: filled circle (user color, 0.18 opacity) + stroke ring (user color, 1.2px). Initials centered in Space Grotesk 700. Idle animation: scale 1→1.25 pulsing loop. Offline blips: gray, opacity 0.4, frozen. Tap → Hero transition to Compass.
MAJOR · SHEET
Bottom sheet: gradient glass, not opaque card
DraggableScrollableSheet minChildSize 0.20. Background: linear-gradient transparent→rgba(10,10,15,0.97) starting at 35%. Radar canvas remains fully visible above fade. Drag handle: 30px pill, 0.18 opacity. Avatar row horizontally scrollable.
POLISH · TOPBAR
Top bar: Stack-positioned, floats over canvas
No AppBar. Positioned inside Stack at top. Session name + count left. Leave/end icon-button right (danger red ring). Transparent background — radar shows through fully.

CRITICAL · NEEDLE
Compass needle: custom polygon, not icon
Two SVG polygons — north (electric blue gradient), south (white 0.06→0.15 opacity). Center hub: outer glow circle (0.3 opacity) + solid dot + hollow core. Rotation via Transform.rotate on the entire SVG element. TweenAnimationBuilder with shortest-path delta.

Tick-mark ring: 72 ticks, cardinal labels
CustomPaint draws 72 ticks at 5° intervals. Major ticks at 45° (1px, 0.4 opacity), minor (0.5px, 0.2). N/S/E/W labels in Space Mono 9px. Outer ring: 0.8px stroke, 0.25 opacity. Inner faint ring at r=72.
MAJOR · ACCURACY
GPS accuracy badge: dot + label top-right
5px filled circle: green=high, amber=medium, red=low. Space Mono label beside it. Updates via geolocator accuracy stream. Only show LOW/MEDIUM prominently — HIGH is quiet.
